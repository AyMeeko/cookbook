These recipes are my baseline for meal prepping lunches each week. The MacroFactor links provided do link directly into my version of the recipe, so I may shift things a bit week over week to reflect the realities of the ingredients on hand. This may result in minor discrepencies between the nutritional information displayed on the recipe page and what's displayed in the MacroFactor app. I recommend importing the recipe and adjusting it in the same manner each time you make the recipe to ensure the nutritional information is as accurate as possible.

Most of these recipes make 4 servings because I've found the most success meal prepping lunches for the week with the idea that Wednesday will be a "figure it out" or "leftover" lunch. It gives me a break in the week so I'm not eating the same thing 5 times in a row, and leaves room for some meals that I enjoy but don't enjoy enough to eat them 4 times a week.
