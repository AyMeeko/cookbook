---
title: Chicken and Cabbage Stir Fry
date: "2026-03-08"
ingredients:
  - 114g Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  - 155g Carrots, chopped
  - 495g Zucchini, sliced in half and then into moons
  - 153g Onions, chopped
  - 600g Green Cabbage, sliced thin like noodles
  - 420g Kirkland Thin Sliced Boneless Chicken Breasts
  - 44g Truvia Brown Sugar
  - 2 tsp Chili Powder
  - 1 tsp Sesame Oil
  - 1 tsp Ginger Root, minced
  - 3 tsp Garlic, minced (or 3 cloves)
  - Spray cooking oil
instructions:
  - Pre-heat medium sized pan to medium-high heat.
  - Season chicken to taste
  - When pan is to temp, add chicken. Let cook without touching for 4 minutes.
  - Cover pan for 2 minutes.
  - Flip chicken and let cook for another 6 minutes. Do not touch it during this time.
  - Using a meat thermometer, check temp has reached between 160 and 165 F.
  - Pull from pan and set aside to rest.
  - In medium bowl, mix brown sugar, soy sauce, chili powder, sesame oil, and ginger, and set aside.
  - Spray very large skillet or wok with cooking oil.
  - Add carrots and cook for 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  - Add zucchini and onion. Cook for 5 more minutes, continuing to stir.
  - Add garlic and cook for 2 more minutes.
  - Remove veggies from pan and set aside.
  - Give the pan another spray of cooking oil.
  - Add cabbage to pan. If it doesn't all fit, add a reasonable amount until it cooks down by half, and then add more.
  - Dice chicken into bite sized pieces while you continue cooking down the cabbage.
  - When cabbage is cooked to your desired tenderness, add back the veggies, your diced chicken, and the sauce.
  - Mix thoroughly, and continue to cook until sauce reaches desired thickness.
