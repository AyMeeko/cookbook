---
title: Beef Chili
date: "2026-03-08"
ingredients:
  - 1 lb Kirkland 9% Fat Lean Ground Beef
  - 479g Kettle & Fire Beef Bone Broth (1 carton)
  - 3 tsp Garlic, minced (or 3 cloves)
  - 2 tbsp Chili Powder
  - 0.5 tsp Cumin Seed
  - 0.5 tbsp Salt
  - 0.5 tsp Black Pepper, ground
  - 0.25 tsp Oregano
  - 0.5 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  - 425g Dark Red Kidney Beans (1 can)
  - 284g Diced Tomatoes
  - 425g Tomato Sauce
  - 142g Onions, diced
instructions:
  - In a large pot, brown ground beef.
  - Add onions and cook until translucent.
  - Add the rest of the ingredients to pot and let simmer.
