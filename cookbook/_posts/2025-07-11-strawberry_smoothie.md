---
title: Strawberry Smoothie
date: "2025-07-11"
ingredients:
  - 100g Dannon Light+Fit Greek Yogurt, Vanilla
  - 100g Kirkland Frozen Strawberries
  - 1 scoop Kirkland Whey Chocolate Protein Powder
  - Cold water to desired consistency
instructions:
  - Place shaker cup on scale and zero it out
  - Add yogurt, strawberries, and some cold water
  - Blend until desired consistency
  - Add the protein powder to blender
  - Pulse a few times until mixed.
  - Drink immediately.
words_of_wisdom:
  - If the protein powder is added at the beginning, it becomes super aerated and thick, which makes it hard to drink. I did not enjoy this.
